Bio

George Ikigu is the founder of Occiris, a speech-to-text startup based in Nairobi, Kenya. He is passionate about helping YouTubers reach a wide and diverse audience through closed captions. He’s especially interested in making online content accessible to hard-of-hearing audiences.

He's a self-taught web developer and works as the primary developer at Occiris. He plays a major role in designing and developing software solutions that meet the company's needs. Currently, he's pursuing the ALX Software Engineering program.
